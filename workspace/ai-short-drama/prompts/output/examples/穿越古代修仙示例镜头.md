## 示例镜头三：晨雾送别

**剧本信息**
- 集数：第1集 幽蓝深渊
- 时长：8秒
- 出场角色：林逸、孙伯
- 情绪：温情

**五维坐标**
```
[镜头3] W1:中景-横移 W2:温情 W3:晨曦金 W4:缓慢 W5:三分
```

**Seedance2 提示词**
```
RAW photo, cinematic medium shot, shot on ARRI Alexa, 35mm lens, 8k resolution,

the subjects: 
- young boy (15 years old, thin, wearing worn brown miner's clothes) pushing open wooden door,
- elderly man (70 years old, hunched back, white hair, leaning on wooden cane) standing in morning mist,
- elderly man handing small cloth bundle to boy,

environment: ancient small town street, simple stone and wood houses lining narrow path,
thick white morning mist hanging at ground level, cobblestone street wet with dew,
simple wooden door with weathered paint, morning sun creating warm backlight through mist,
ancient well with stone rim in background, faint smoke from cooking fires rising,

lighting & atmosphere:
- soft golden early morning sunlight filtering through mist,
- warm light on elderly man's face creating rim light through mist,
- cool blue-grey tones in shaded areas,
- mist creating soft volumetric atmosphere,
- dust particles visible in sunlight beams,

composition: two shot with boy on left third, elder on right third,
camera slowly tracking laterally following interaction,
background houses creating depth layers,

technical: high dynamic range, film grain, warm emotional atmosphere,
soft focus background, photorealistic textures

negative: CGI, cartoon, anime, oversaturated, bright harsh lighting, text, watermark
```

**Character Reference**: 
- 林逸 普通状态（generate.py character --id lin_yi --state normal）
